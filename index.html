Frankendelta

2019

Meet the 45lb, reassembled many times, absolute unit of a Delta 3D Printer: Frankendelta. Over the summer of 2019, my dad and I worked together to design a 3D printer with a build volume large enough to print my head (that wasn't the main goal... but it's on the list). In all fairness, my dad brought in most of the expertise from building his Kossel Mini Delta 3D printer, scaling up the things he felt worked well from that. I had experience with cartesian printers, but deltas are a different beast. With the right settings, they can print extremely quickly without quality loss due to their light effectors.
